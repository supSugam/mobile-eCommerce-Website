"Technology & Daily Life"

To many people, technology is amazing as it enables us to connect with people around the world and make a difference in everyday life in all aspects of society. But to others, it is a nightmare, a pathway to a destructive future where humans have become dependent on machines.

Technology has had a huge impact on everyone in our society, and that doesn't excludes me either. In my opinion, both positive and negative side of technology are equally precise. I stand by both sides of it. Technlogoy has had a huge impact on me since childhood. Technology has pushed me to spend half of my childhood indoor while I was supposed to be socializing outside. Technology surely has more of a negative impact on people who don't know how to gain its advantage in proper way.

But in the positive side, technology is a friend of every human. They make everything too convenient. It's evolving everyday. It has become a source of entertainment to billions of people. Evolving technolody indeed has saved millions of human life too. I love positive side of technology more than I hate its negative side.
